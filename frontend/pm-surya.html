What is PM Surya Ghar Muft Bijli Yojna?

PM Surya Ghar: Muft Bijli Yojana (PMSGMBY) is a national initiative by the Government of India aimed at connecting 1 crore households to solar energy and enabling up to 300 units of free electricity per month. Under this scheme, a grid-connected rooftop solar system is installed on the house roof, enabling consumers to:

  • Generate their own electricity
  • Reduce their electricity bill to nearly zero
  • Earn from surplus power through net metering

Shri Solar (Shri Badrinath Vaikalpik Urja Limited) is officially registered under this scheme in Uttarakhand, Uttar Pradesh, Madhya Pradesh, and Odisha, and provides complete end-to-end services to customers.

Key Features & Benefits

Up to 300 Units Free Electricity

A 3 kW rooftop solar system generates approximately 300 units per month, making the electricity bill nearly zero.

Central + State Government Subsidy

Subsidy is transferred directly to the consumer’s bank account through DBT (Direct Benefit Transfer).

Who is Eligible?

  • Must be an Indian citizen with a residential electricity connection.
  • Must have a concrete roof with sufficient shadow-free area (approx. 100 sq.ft per kW).
  • The applicant must not have already availed a solar subsidy for the same premises.
