Our Story

Shri Solar, legally known as Shri Badrinath Alternative Energy Limited, is a pioneer in the Indian renewable energy sector. For over two decades, we have been committed to providing affordable and sustainable solar solutions across India.

Founded in 2005, we began with a vision to bring clean energy to every household and business. Today, we are a Public Limited Company with a presence in multiple states, empowering thousands of customers with cutting-edge Solar PV and Thermal technologies.

20+ Years of Excellence

With two decades of experience, we have successfully completed over 400+ large-scale projects and served 10,000+ happy customers.
