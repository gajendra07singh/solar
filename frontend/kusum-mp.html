What is PM-KUSUM Scheme?

The Pradhan Mantri Kisan Urja Suraksha evam Utthaan Mahabhiyan (PM-KUSUM) is a transformative initiative by the Government of India. In Madhya Pradesh, it is managed by MPUVNL (MP Urja Vikas Nigam Limited) with the goal of providing reliable daytime power for irrigation and extra income for farmers.

Mission 2026

The scheme has been extended until March 2026 with an allocation of ₹3,000 crore to accelerate solar adoption in MP's farm sector.

Three Components of KUSUM

Component A: Solar Plants

Set up small solar plants (500 kW to 2 MW) on barren or fallow land. Sell power to DISCOMs or lease land for ₹60k–1L per acre/year.

Component B: Solar Pumps

Get standalone solar pumps (up to 7.5 HP) for off-grid areas. Highly subsidized for individual farmers.

Component C: Solarization

Solarize existing grid-connected pumps. Use solar power for irrigation and sell surplus back to the grid.

Feeder Solarization

Large scale solarization of agricultural feeders to provide 24/7 free solar power to entire farming clusters.

Subsidies in Madhya Pradesh

Madhya Pradesh offers some of the highest subsidies for solar pumps, particularly under the PM Krishak Mitra Surya Yojana.

2 HP Solar Pump Farmer Share: ~₹23,000*
3 HP Solar Pump Farmer Share: ~₹36,000*
5 HP Solar Pump Farmer Share: ~₹72,000*
7.5 HP Solar Pump Farmer Share: ~₹1,35,000*

*Approximate values. Certain categories receive up to 90% total subsidy.

Eligibility & Documents

Individual farmers, cooperatives, Panchayats, and Farmer Producer Organizations (FPOs) can apply.

Land Records

Khasra/Khatauni maps showing ownership of cultivable or barren land.

KYC Documents

Aadhaar Card, Mobile Number (linked), and Bank Passbook for subsidy DBT.

How to Apply?

Shri Solar provides complete assistance for the KUSUM application process in MP:

  • Step 1: Online registration on the CM Solar Pump Portal or MPUVNL website.
  • Step 2: Payment of registration fee (if applicable).
  • Step 3: Land and site verification by government officials.
  • Step 4: Deposit of farmer's share after approval.
  • Step 5: Installation and Commissioning by Shri Solar team.
