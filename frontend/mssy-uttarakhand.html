Mukhyamantri Saur Swarojgar Yojana (MSSY)

The Mukhyamantri Saur Swarojgar Yojana (MSSY) is a flagship initiative by the Government of Uttarakhand designed to promote renewable energy adoption while fostering sustainable livelihoods. The scheme empowers local residents to become solar power producers, contributing to the state's green energy goals while securing a stable financial future.

Primary Objectives

  • Self-Employment: Creating sustainable business opportunities for youth and farmers within the state.
  • Energy Generation: Developing utility-scale, ground-mounted solar power plants.
  • Revenue Model: Enabling beneficiaries to sell generated electricity to UPCL through long-term agreements.

Who Can Apply?

The scheme is open to the following categories of individuals residing in Uttarakhand:

Local Youth

Local youth seeking sustainable self-employment opportunities within their home state.

Farmers

Farmers and agricultural landowners looking to diversify their income sources.

Entrepreneurs

Budding entrepreneurs and landowners with vacant or unused land parcels.

Subsidy and Financial Support

The program is backed by state government incentives to lower the barrier to entry for local residents.

Government Subsidy

State subsidies significantly reduce project cost and initial equity contribution.

Bank Loan Assistance

Facilitation of low-interest loans through nationalized and cooperative banks.

25-Year PPA

Revenue is secured via a 25-year Power Purchase Agreement with UPCL.

Project Scale & Land Requirement

MSSY supports various scales of utility-scale ground-mounted solar systems.

Minimum Land

Typically starts from 1 acre for smaller installations, making it accessible for small landowners.

Revenue Potential

Projects up to 1 MW can generate an estimated annual revenue of ₹8–15 lakhs.

Required Documents & Approvals

Setting up a project under MSSY requires several technical and administrative clearances:

MSSY Portal Application & Registration
Techno-Feasibility Report (TFR)
Clearance from various state departments (NOCs)
Electrical Safety & Fire Dept. Clearance
Grid Integration Documentation
