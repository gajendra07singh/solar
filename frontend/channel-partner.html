Our Partnership Model

A collaborative approach to solarizing 1 crore households across North India.

What Shri Solar Provides

  • Customer billing & Government registration
  • Plant installation & Technical support
  • Bank loan processing for customers
  • Sales leads & Promotional materials
  • Dedicated Sales Manager & TSM support
  • Full product warranties

What the Partner Does

  • Customer communication & Lead generation
  • Document collection for registration
  • Coordinate with Electricity Dept & Banks
  • Follow company price list & policies
  • Daily reporting to Sales Manager

Incentive Structure

Our incentive model is designed to reward high performance and transparency.

  • Application & Verification

    Leads are assigned or generated. Documents are verified for the PM Surya Ghar portal.

  • Site Survey & Quote

    Our team conducts the survey. Partner facilitates customer interaction.

  • Installation & Commissioning

    System is installed. Net-metering is completed.

  • Payout

    Incentives are paid out based on completed units and extra billing shares.
