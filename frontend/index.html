Explore Our Innovative Solar Solutions

Solar Street Lights

Enjoy smart, eco-friendly illumination with our advanced solar street lights featuring automatic dusk-to-dawn sensors, high-capacity lithium batteries, and weather-proof IP65 housing for reliable performance in all conditions.

Solar Power Plant

Maximize your energy independence with custom-engineered solar power plants. We provide turnkey solutions for rooftop and ground-mounted systems, ensuring high efficiency, rapid ROI, and zero carbon footprint.

High Mast & Flag Mast

Illuminate large public spaces, junctions, and stadiums with our robust solar high mast systems. These structures are integrated with high-lumen solar LEDs and remote monitoring for maximum safety and visibility.

LED AC Lights

Upgrade to the future of lighting with our energy-saving LED AC solutions. Designed for high durability and maximum brightness, these lights reduce energy consumption by up to 80% while offering a significantly longer lifespan.

Solar Energy Storage System (SESS)

Store your excess solar energy for use during nights or power cuts. Our SESS features smart hybrid technology with long-cycle life batteries to provide seamless, 24/7 power backup for homes and industries.

Solar Water Pump

Transform your agriculture with our high-efficiency solar water pumping systems. Available in surface and submersible models, they provide reliable irrigation without the need for expensive diesel or grid power.

Solar Home Lighting

Bring clean light to every corner with our portable and decentralized solar home lighting kits. Includes high-efficiency solar panels, multiple LED bulbs, fans, and mobile charging ports for off-grid living comfort.

Solar Water Heater

Get consistent hot water at zero cost with our solar thermal systems. Utilizing ETC and FPC technology, these heaters provide high-temperature water even in cloudy weather and cold winters.

Solar Inverters

Bridge the gap between solar panels and your appliances with our high-performance MPPT solar inverters. Features include pure sine wave output, smart LCD displays, and comprehensive protection against surges.
